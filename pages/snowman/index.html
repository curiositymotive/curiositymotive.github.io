Many of us at Curiosity Motive grew up watching the animated version of Raymond Brigg's "The Snowman" each year around the holidays. We wanted to pay tribute to the animated short's amazing flying scenes by allowing you to take a short flight of your own with the Snowman using A-Frame and WebVR. Use a mobile compatible VR headset or even tilt your phone or use your mouse to soar through the countryside to Monument Circle in our hometown of Indianapolis.

"I remember that winter because it had brought the heaviest snow I had ever seen. Snow had fallen steadily all night long and in the morning I woke in a room filled with light and silence, the whole world seemed to be held in a dream-like stillness. It was a magical day... and it was on that day I made the Snowman."
-- Raymond Briggs, The Snowman
